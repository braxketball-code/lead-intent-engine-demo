# Paste into proposals / follow-ups

---

**Demo link:** https://github.com/braxketball-code/lead-intent-engine-demo

I put together a working demo of the exact architecture I described:

- Multi-source lead ingest (forms, scrapers, social monitors)
- 0–100 fit scoring with reasoning + tags
- Hot/warm/cool/cold routing with recommended next actions
- Exportable JSON/CSV for CRM or Sheets
- Importable n8n workflow template (webhook → score → Slack/CRM route)

Sample run surfaces hot leads like pre-approved buyers and high-budget automation buyers automatically — low-intent "just curious" leads get archived.

Happy to share the repo, screen-share a 5-min walkthrough, or adapt this to your vertical in V1.
